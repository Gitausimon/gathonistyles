# Style by Gathoni

## Why I built this
I built this for my mom because I saw she was struggling with keeping track of the orders and replying to the customers to update them every step of the way. 

## What it does
* **Catalogue:** Shows the catalogue of the clothes and their prices.
* **Measurement Guides:** Includes videos to direct her customers on how to take their measurements accurately.
* **Admin Panel:** Contains her admin panel where she can customize everything on her own.
* **Estimated Wait Time:** Helps the customers know the estimated wait time for their orders.
* **Order Tracking:** Keeps track of incoming orders and customer details.
* **Customer Communication:** Has pre-written WhatsApp replies for updates, making it easy to keep clients informed without typing repetitive messages.

## Tech Stack
Since this was a simple project, I built it using:
* Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS
* Firebase (for Auth and Backend)
